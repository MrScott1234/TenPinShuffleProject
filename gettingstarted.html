Getting Started

The first step is to download a copy of 10 Pin Shuffle from the Google Play Store.

 

Next you'll need to grab the .APK file for 10 Pin Shuffle and extract the contents into a folder somewhere on your computer. You can do this using any off the shelf .APK extractor app.

 

Once all of that is done, you'll simply need to extract the contents using a program like 7zip. To do this; right click the .APK file, go to 7zip, and click "Extract Files".
